Kawasaki Ninja H2 R

Supercharged Supremacy: 998cc Inline-Four with 321 HP, Built to Redefine Speed and Power.

The Kawasaki Ninja H2 R boasts a 998cc inline-four engine with dual overhead cams and 16 valves, equipped with a centrifugal supercharger developed in-house by Kawasaki Heavy Industries. This engine delivers an incredible 321.5 horsepower at 14,000 rpm, making it one of the most powerful production bikes. It uses a dog-ring six-speed gearbox for lightning-fast gear changes.

The H2 R uses an IMU-based system to manage multiple electronic aids, including traction control, launch control, engine braking control, and cornering ABS. It also features a quick shifter for seamless gear changes and an electronically adjustable steering damper for added stability.

The frame is a steel trellis design optimized for high-speed stability. The rear features a single-sided swingarm, and suspension is handled by a fully adjustable KYB AOS-II front fork and an Öhlins TTX36 rear shock. Brembo Stylema brake calipers and lightweight aluminum wheels enhance stopping power and performance.

Carbon fiber winglets and a fully enclosed aerodynamic body give the H2 R exceptional downforce at high speed. The aerodynamic design was developed in collaboration with Kawasaki’s aerospace division, maximizing stability and airflow.
